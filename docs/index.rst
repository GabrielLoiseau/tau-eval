Welcome to tau-eval's documentation!
====================================

**Tau-Eval** is a user-friendly, modular, and customizable Python library designed to benchmark and evaluate text anonymization algorithms. It enables granular analysis of anonymization impacts from both privacy and utility perspectives. Tau-Eval seamlessly integrates with [LiteLLM](https://www.litellm.ai/) and [ðŸ¤— Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/) to support a wide range of datasets, models, and evaluation metrics.
